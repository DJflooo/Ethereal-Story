Chapter 1 Cast:

Lucan Ether: The Protagonist

A young man who awakens on a beach with no memory of his past. He quickly learns about his Plasma magic and already meets a friend: Morden. Morden tells him about their past: they were captured and experimented on by an organization. He then quickly leaves the beach, getting attacked by an enormous monster called "White Eyes". He is then saved by a boat from a faction calling themselves the Lightbringers.
Appearance: Rags, facial features unknown
Magic: Plasma

Morden: The Old Friend

Another young man who also woke up on the beach alongside Lucan. He seems to know more about their situation, telling Lucan that they were both captured by an organization and experimented on. He stays on the island Lucan woke up on while Lucan leaves to explore the world and try to regain his memories.
Appearance: Rags, midly short messy black hair, deep blue eyes
Magic: Unknown/None

Riley: The Vice-Captain of the Lightbringers | The Lightbringers

One of the vice-captains of the Lightbringers and an Amber Torch, she is extremely skilled for her age and travels with her friend Temia. She seems nice and helps Lucan. Her crew is also composed of Gramih, a large man obsessed with fishing, and Bert, a skilled warrior using a longsword, among many others.
Appearance:
  • Riley: Long blond hair with some of strands a fiery red, wearing a purple sweater, yellow armbands and long brown pants and the Lightbringers' badge
  • Temia: Short brown hair tied in a small ponytail, wearing a slightly dimmer yellow tank top and dark-purple shorts
  • Gramih: Very large and muscular, bald with a thick brown beard, wearing a sleeveless dark-purple tunic and light-blue pants
  • Bert: Tall and lean, short spiky dark-purple hair, wearing a full set of purple-ish armor, the symbol of the Lightbringers on his chest, near his heart
